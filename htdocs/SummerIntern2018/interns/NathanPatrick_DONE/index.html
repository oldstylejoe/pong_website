Who am I?

A creative, logical, person seeking answers to life, the universe, and everything.

I have a Bachelor's in Business Management from San Diego State University, graduated June of 2017. I have worked on various projects in business courses such as:

Creativity in business, where I created an idea to help people learn a language by through immersive interaction with characters in a foreign country as a police investigator by speaking to them through a computer microphone.

International Business, where my team analyzed the viability of entering into India and what strategies and cultural differences would need to be taken into account investing in India.

Business Strategy, where my team formulated where the Campbell Soup Company, based on a case study, should proceed next to help recover from a slump in profits, such as new products? Reinvent old ones? Cull back their offerings and focus on the core market?

I am currently studying programming at the PoNG center Internship, designing video games with a purpose of doing more than to just entertain. My team's project was to develop a mobile app in Unity to help individuals with dementia slow down the effects of dementia through walking to places on interest.

Some of my many passions include music, art, and science.

Motivated

I always strive to do my best, seeking to do the job right. If you aren't willing to put your all into something that needs your all, why? Always put your all into it!

Creative

The box is an illusion. If you think outside the box, You're still confined to that box. I have broken free from the illusion that the box exists. Everyone can "think outside the box" if they try.

Methodical

The key to a successfully solving a problem where the end answer is known, is not to begin at the start of the problem and work forward, but to start at the end of the project and work backward. Think logically, and the solution will reveal itself.

Passionate

I have many passions and wish to understand all things. I play music, learn about advances in science, read about the state of the world, and study "how-to's" on most everything.
