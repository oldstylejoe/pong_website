HACKATHON - JUNE 22-23, 2017, ATKINSON HALL

MAKING GAMES WITH MOVEMENT
Experience is the best teacher, and hackathons are all about experience. In this hackathon weekend, particpants will use eye and movement sensors to create games. Prizes will be awarded to teams that demonstrate the most effective use of motion controls, the most creative use of motion controls, and the best game using motion controls.
